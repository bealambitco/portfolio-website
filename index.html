Work & Life

Bea Lambitco is the Head of Data and Analytics at ATR Asset Management (ATRAM), a leading independent asset management firm in the Philippines, with over six years of experience in data and analytics. She spearheaded the establishment of the company’s Data team, driving informed decision-making and operational efficiency through the implementation of a data warehouse. Currently, she's focused on deepening analytics capabilities and driving innovation through data science and AI initiatives. Prior to her role, Bea handled Risk Analytics, expanding its reach by engaging external clients for risk and advisory services.

Committed to professional growth, Bea holds certifications including Financial Risk Manager from the Global Association of Risk Professionals and Microsoft Azure Data and AI Engineer. As a passionate advocate for data and AI, she actively participates in social impact data projects, such as the AI-driven disaster assistance chatbot by Omdena, and continues to upskill through hackathons and AI courses. Beyond her own development, Bea pays it forward by mentoring aspiring data professionals and nurturing future leaders through various platforms, including Women in Big Data, FutureLab, and Data Engineering Pilipinas. She graduated Magna Cum Laude from Ateneo de Manila University with a Master's Degree in Applied Mathematics. Outside of work, she enjoys outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and yoga.
